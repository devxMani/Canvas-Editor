About Canvas Editor

Welcome to Canvas Editor, a versatile tool for creating digital designs with text and images.

Features:

  • Text editing with customizable font size, color, and style
  • Background color selection and image upload
  • Text formatting options (bold, italic, uppercase, lowercase)
  • Undo and redo functionality for easy editing
  • Export your designs as PDF files
  • Drag and drop text positioning

How to Use:

  1. Use the text input field to enter your desired text
  2. Adjust font size, color, and style using the provided controls
  3. Click and drag to position your text on the canvas
  4. Experiment with background colors or upload an image
  5. Use the undo/redo buttons to revise your work
  6. When finished, download your creation as a PDF

Frequently Asked Questions

To change the font size, use the size dropdown menu located in the control panel. Select the desired size from the available options.

To undo a change, click the undo button (circular arrow pointing counterclockwise) in the toolbar. You can undo multiple actions by clicking the button repeatedly.
